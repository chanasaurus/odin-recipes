Arancini

Description

This recipe is from Recipe Tin Eats.

I need this recipe because we need to make a dish starting with the first letter of our name for Friendsmas.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Risotto
  • White Wine
  • Chicken Broth
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Cheese
  • Parsley
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Flour
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven.
  2. Melt butter.
  3. Add rice and wine.
  4. Add chicken broth and milk.
  5. Remove from oven and allow to cool.
  6. Serve with marinara sauce.
